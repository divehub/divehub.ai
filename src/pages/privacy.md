# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 9, 2025

DiveHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the information we collect when you use the DiveHub application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect any personal data.**

DiveHub is designed to be a private, offline-first experience. We do not collect, store, share, or process any personal information, usage data, or analytics. All data created within the app (such as dive plans, profiles, and settings) is stored securely on your local device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## 2. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
